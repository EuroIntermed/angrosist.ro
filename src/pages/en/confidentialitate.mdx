## 1. Who we are

Angrosist.ro is a B2B sourcing channel operated in the EURO INTERMED SOLUTIONS ecosystem, Reg. No.: J8/735/2018, VAT: 39132147, Brașov, Romania. EURO INTERMED SOLUTIONS is the controller of the data submitted through this website.

For questions about personal data, contact us at eurointermeds@gmail.com or +40 745 79 99 95.

## 2. What data we collect

We only collect the information you choose to send us via WhatsApp, the AI assistant, email, phone or Calendly. This may include:

- the company name and country;
- the contact person's name;
- email, phone / WhatsApp and website;
- the request route: product sourcing, recurring supply, product list, supplier offer, clearance redirect or another B2B opportunity;
- initial details about product, category, quantity, delivery, availability or the free-text message you send.

We do not ask for file uploads, VAT numbers, tax documents or full commercial terms at the first contact.

## 3. Why we use the data

We use the data to qualify the B2B sourcing request, to contact you about it and to route it to the right channel in the Euro Intermed Solutions ecosystem.

The legal bases are your consent for contact and our legitimate interest in operating a B2B commercial service.

## 4. Who we share data with

The data stays within EURO INTERMED SOLUTIONS and the relevant B2B channels. We do not publish the request, the company name or commercial details without your consent, and we do not sell personal data.

We may use service providers such as email, WhatsApp, telephony or Calendly for communication. We may disclose data only if required by law.

## 5. How long we keep data

We keep data only as long as necessary to handle the request, for a resulting commercial relationship, or until you ask for deletion or withdraw your consent, unless the law requires otherwise.

## 6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability and withdrawal of consent. To exercise your rights, write to eurointermeds@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ANSPDCP.

We may update this policy. The current version is shown on this page.
